The Voice of Indie Games

At Babycastles Gallery in NYC

In 2009, Babycastles established the world’s first dedicated video game exhibit in a basement in Queens, New York City. Since, the video game and design collective have opened a permanent space in Manhattan: Babycastles Gallery in 2014. Babycastles Gallery is a hub for game designers, developers, artists, curators and other creators to collaborate, test, critique and create community. Now, Babycastles is an internationally recognized organization looking to identify new voices in game creation and provide them with exposure to new audiences.

The Cool

Babycastles is a hybrid gallery, event, arcade, co-working space, game studio and even pop-up yoga studio at times. The collective has produced game exhibitions in partnership with art institutions like the MoMA, American Museum of Natural History, and the Brooklyn Academy of Music. The true beauty of the space is the rich community of residents, exhibitors, and visitors that engage with the daily ongoings of the gallery. Babycastles is an important site of indie innovation and collaboration in New York City. If you’re a fan of experimental and independent games make sure to visit the Babycastles Gallery in Manhattan.
